INTRODUCTION

The most recent and sole licensed regional chapter to a global organization network of Mind-Body training systems, Health as well as Education based Programs present in over 22 countries and coming from South Korea with close to 40 years of culminated research and experience in the field of health-science, happiness and peace. The BE-ME program, provided by a Qatar-based corporation (Think Opposite WLL) follows a wide network of Institutions and Educators in the areas of child development, school student character building programs, under and post-graduate facilities, community based training centers, executive private and company education, Special Needs Brain clinics as well as in-need UNESCO tied National Care and Development programs, actively supporting UN efforts to promote international peace.

Brain Education is system of training and philosophy that helps self-discover, self-manage, and self-explore creatively in a way not only helpful to manage change inside individuals, but around them too. BE-ME help in creating the life individuals wish to live and be of benefit not only to themselves but everyone else too, so they can become a true human character, one that takes care of him or herself and others and widely benefit humanity.
